Protection Provisions of Children

1. Counseller

2. Care and Protection of Children

All students of school are protected as per Protection of children from sexual offences Act 2012 and Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act 2000. Teaching and Non Teaching staff members are not accused of any crime under aforementioned acts or any other acts.

3. Prohibition of Tobacco and it's Products

Advertisement, Sale, production and distribution of Cigarette and Tobacco products is prohibited in 100 meters periphery of School. In School campus also Tobacco and it's products are prohibited. School is free from Tobacco and it's products.
