Admission and Withdrawal Rules

Parents must be given to understand that they cannot dictate to the management, but that the management has the right to state the conditions under which they will admit or retain pupils in their school.

No one is to be admitted without a transfer certificate, report card and conduct certificate from the school last attended.

The parent/guardian should fill in the school Admission form with utmost accuracy. The signing of the Declaration form will constitute an agreement on the part of the parent/guardian and the students, to abide by the rules of the school.

No transfer certificate will be issued till all dues are paid. Such a certificate will only be given during office working hours and two days after the application has been submitted.

No certificate of any kind will be issued without prior application by the parent. A character certificate will be issued on the basis of his/her regularity, punctuality, behaviour and the pursuit of values gained in school.
