A BRIEF HISTORY OF THE SCHOOL

A man, who treated with humility to down trodden people, poor and scheduled tribes was Amrit Lal Vitthal Das Thakkar "Bapa". He was an extra ordinary man who devoted his whole life like as a candle which lights others in consuming itself. Ex-Voice Prime Minister Shri Jagjiwan Ram said that that Bapa was Dadhichi of modern age. He made a union fo Gonds and performed a society named of GOND SEWAK SANGH. Registered of Society Act XXI Of 1860 NO 37 OF 1945-1946. After some years passed on 10 March 1949 has changed its name The VANVASI SEWA MANDAL MANDLA under the society Registeration Act XXI Of 1860. Shri Amrit Lal Vitthal Das Thakkar was elected as the Director fo this society. The highest function fo his motto was to get up wretched people,poor,scheduled tribes with emotional excitting in humility.

Shri Jagpati Singh Thakur ex-M.L.A. of Badwara constituency met to the Secretary of this institution shri Narayan Vinayak Bapat, Who was freedom fighter of our country. Shri Bapat ji accepted the request of shri Jagpati Singh Thakur to open a Middle School in his village Vilayatkalan Dist. Katni in 1954. The Incharge head master was Shri Nand Kishor Gupta and he devoted his entire life with his fulfilment. He started 5th class in 1954. The building has a room with two varandahs and attached with a hostel. A plot about 1.85 hectare has been donated for School accomodation and also for hostel by shri Jagpati Thakur addressee to Vilayatkalan and for its economical income he had denoted a agricultural land of 2.83 hactare.

The first batch of this institution Board of 7th class appeared in 1957. The son of Seth Govind Das - Shri Jagmohan Das who was a minister of the M.P. Ministary of Bhopal came Vilayatkalan, On request of shri thakur Jagpati Singh and sanctioned to upgrade with class 8th to 10th in 1959. Shri G.P.Trivedi became Principal of Bapu Vanvasi Sewa Mandal High School Vilayatkalan. In 1961 Middle School was handed over its charge to tribel welfare department by Society(Vanvasi Sewa Mandal Mandla). The students of first batch of High School appeared in High School Board Exam in 1960 and passed with the result of 100%. The institution was upgraded in higher secodary School by untiring efforts of Shri Thakur Jagpati Singh who was chairman of Bapu Vanvasi Sewa Mandal Hr. Sec. School - Vilayatkaln. The first batch of this institution of 19 students appeared in Higher Secondary School certificate examination in 1962-63 and passed whole students with centpercent result. The students showed their good performance of studies in this rural area. Shri Thakur Nasib Singh was the principal of Bapu Vanvasi Sewa Mandal Hr.Sec.School - Vilayatkalan. The institution has increased its stature by leaps and bounds,with good performance and untirng efforts of Thakur Jagpati Singh. Centpercent Grant in aid was provided to this institution by M.P. Government.

Shri Thakur Jagpati Singh devoted his entire life to development of wretched poor people scheduled tribes and scheduled castes. The institution Bapu Vanvasi Sewa Mandal Hr. Sec. School - Vilayatkalan was bacome famous in the field fo well education and its cultural programme. Shri M.D. Sant bacame Principal in 01/06/1964. Our students have brought rich laurels with good image to this institution. The students participated in inter school debates and took part in games competitions and were awarded in the field of educational and cultural activities.

The school has a good library, science laboratory and class rooms with furniture. It has big play ground and greenary garden.

Now Shri A.K.Patel is the Incharge Principal of Bapu Vanvasi Sewa Mandal Hr.Sec.School - Vilayatklalan with effect from 01/03/2014 to till now.
