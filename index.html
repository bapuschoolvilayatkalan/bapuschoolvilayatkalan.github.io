Welcome to Bapu Vanvasi Sewa Mandal Higher Secondary School-Vilayatkalan

Bapu Vanvasi Sewa Mandal Higher Secondary School-Vilayatkalan, Katni was started in the year 1954 with classes from V to Class VII. Classes were upgraded upto class X in 1959. Shri G.P.Trivedi was the Principal of the School. Again in the year 1962-63 it was upgraded as Higher Secondary School.

The School now a Higher Secondary school affiliated to the Board of Secondary Education, Madhya Pradesh Bhopal.

The School is managed by Madhya Pradesh Vanvasi Sewa Mandal Mandla. Mr Kailash Chand Jain senior social worker from Dindori is our Chairman.

100 % Grant in aid is given to this school by the Government of Madhya Pradesh.

Our Motto

The aims and objectives of our motto are to literate the poor who are down trodden and wretched people. May the Almighty God guide and inspire us in this great endeavour of services toward the Institution with humility. The chief wonder in it learn and teach. Let our teaching be full of ideas. Hitherto it has been stuffed only with facts, because we know learning is the age of the mind and wisdom is the health of soul. The love of reading enables a man to exchange the wearisome hours of life, which comes to every one for hours of delight.
